Find My Phone by Clap

About the App

The "Find My Phone by Clap" app helps you locate your phone at home by responding to two claps. Simply clap your hands twice, and the phone will emit a sound, even if it's in silent mode.

Privacy Policy

We collect and use minimal data to ensure the app's functionality. The app requests microphone access to respond to clapping sounds and does not record any audio files.

We use AdMob to display ads. Please refer to Privacy Policy for more information.
